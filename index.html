📖 How to read NSE contract names:
ATGL + 12MAR26 + 480 + CE  =  Stock Symbol + Expiry Date + Strike Price + Option Type
CE = Call Option (buy if price goes UP)
PE = Put Option (buy if price goes DOWN)
ATM = At the Money (near current price)
ITM = In the Money (profitable now)
OTM = Out of the Money (need price to move)
